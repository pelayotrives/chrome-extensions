How it works

  1. Create groups to match your workflows. Call them Work, Dev, AI, Writing, whatever fits.
  2. Assign each extension to a group using the dropdown next to its name, or drag and drop it onto a group chip.
  3. Right click a supergroup to enter edit mode and add or remove groups inside it.
  4. Toggle groups instantly with the power button. One click disables everything you don't need right now.
  5. Snapshots let you freeze a full extension state and jump back to it later. Perfect for switching contexts.
