About Extensio Patronus

Your extension management hub. Group, control, and snapshot your extensions. All from one compact popup.

Getting started

  1. Create groups - Organize extensions by context (Work, Dev, AI, etc.).
  2. Assign extensions - Use the dropdown in each row to place an extension into a group.
  3. Toggle groups - Click the power button on any group chip to enable/disable all its extensions at once.
  4. Snapshots - Save your current state (groups, assignments, on/off) and restore it later.
